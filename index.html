Scrum Master Involvement Across Stages

How the Scrum Master's role evolves as teams grow in maturity‚Äîfrom hands-on facilitation to autonomous agility.

Scrum Master vs Team Involvement

Visualizing the shift in responsibilities across the development lifecycle.

Stage 1: Onboarding

Scrum Master involvement is high ‚Äî facilitating ceremonies, introducing agile tools, and nurturing trust.

  • Facilitates stand-ups, planning, retrospectives
  • Introduces agile tools and rituals
  • Builds trust and psychological safety

Stage 2: Coaching

Scrum Master involvement shifts to moderate ‚Äî guiding and mentoring as the team gains ownership.

  • Team leads ceremonies with light guidance
  • Focus on agile maturity and velocity
  • Scrum Master supports growth and reflection

Stage 3: Strategic Advisory

Scrum Master involvement becomes strategic ‚Äî focusing on alignment, scaling, and cross-team agility.

  • Team self-organizes fully
  • Scrum Master focuses on strategy and scaling
  • Emphasis on continuous improvement
